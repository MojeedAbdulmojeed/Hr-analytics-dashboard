#**Employee Metrics Dashboard**
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of an HR dataset using **Excel** for data cleaning and **Power BI** for visualization and dashboard development. The goal is to uncover insights related to employee engagement, training outcomes, performance ratings, and departmental metrics.

---

## **Project Overview**

The **Employee Metrics Dashboard** offers a centralized view of key HR indicators to support data-driven workforce decisions. It highlights patterns in employee engagement, training participation, departmental distribution, and performance outcomes.

This project showcases skills in:

* Data cleaning and preparation (Excel)
* Data modeling and visualization (Power BI)
* Insight extraction and dashboard storytelling

---

## **Tools & Technologies**

* **Excel** – data cleaning and transformation
* **Power BI** – data modeling, DAX, and interactive dashboard creation

---

## **Key Insights**

* The **Executive Office** recorded the **highest average engagement score** at **3.38**, which is **16.12% higher** than the **Production** department — the lowest at **2.91**.
* Engagement scores across all six departments ranged from **2.91 to 3.38**, showing moderate variation.
* The **High** performance category accounted for **91%** of total employees.
* **Incomplete** training had the highest number of employees at **775**, followed by Completed, Passed, and Failed outcomes.
* For **Internal** training, **13.70%** of employees successfully completed their training.
* The **Incomplete** category also recorded the highest **average employee count (387.50)** among training outcomes.
* The biggest sentiment gap in training occurred during **External** sessions, where **Neutral sentiment** exceeded **Positive sentiment** by **1,225 employees**.
